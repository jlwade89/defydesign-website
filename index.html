FAQ

Before you order.

Can you create the card design for me?+

Yes. Send your logo, a rough sketch, or your contact information. Design services can be quoted and included before production.

Why is the engraving silver on colored aluminum?+

The laser removes the anodized coating and exposes the natural silver aluminum underneath.

How does stainless steel engrave?+

Brushed stainless is marked with a dark black or charcoal laser mark and is priced $2.00 higher per card than the matching aluminum configuration.

Can every employee have different information?+

Yes. Team orders with multiple employee variations can be organized and quoted before production.
