FAQ

Before you order.

Can you create the design for me?+

Yes. Send your logo and contact information, and Defy Design can prepare a clean layout for approval.

Can both sides be engraved?+

Yes. Single-sided and double-sided layouts are available depending on the finish and design.

Can you add a QR code?+

Yes. QR codes can link to your website, digital contact card, booking page, social profile, or another destination.

Do you support company and team orders?+

Yes. Bulk orders and multiple employee variations can be quoted for teams and recurring business customers.
